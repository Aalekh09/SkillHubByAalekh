Who We Are

SkillHub by Aalekh is a student-led platform offering coding education, design resources, and digital solutions. Founded by Aalekh Kumar, it aims to make learning simple, creative, and accessible.

We believe in empowering students with the skills they need to succeed in the digital world. Through interactive teaching, professional design templates, and custom freelance solutions, we help students transform their ideas into reality.
